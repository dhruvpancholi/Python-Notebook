Mission

Your Mission is to control the resistanceof the segments, so that the connectedbubbles are relatively farther and facilitating the thread to turn red.

The four buttons provided in each loop can be used to control the resistance, Uper 2 to increase the resistance and lower 2 to decrease the resistance.

Cutting the thread would add 5 points to your score and you will be provided with 3 lives.

Warning: Bubbles can reconnect if they comes closer.

Have Fun!!
